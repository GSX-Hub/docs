    GSX Hub

    An installation / manager tool for GSX Profiles.

    Download Docs

    Why GSX Hub?

    Cut out the unnecessary work of installing profiles and enjoy more flying in MSFS.

    Instant Updates

    Keep your profiles up-to-date with ease. Just how the creators intended!

    One-Click Download

    No need to download .zip files anymore, one-click download straight to your GSX folder.

    Automatic Recommendations

    Not sure which profile you need for a scenery? We'll show you!

    Fostering a community of creators

    Add your profile to the GSX Hub and let users download it with a single click. We've explained how to upload and maintain through a simple step-by-step process.

    Easy Profile Management

    Easily update compatible profiles or remove them from your folder entirely.
